How It Works

From choosing a vehicle to delivery at Lagos port, we simplify the entire process for you.
Heres how DirecTokunbo helps you buy confidently from China.

Browse & Inspect

Explore over 500,000 auction vehicles. View detailed specs, condition reports, engine photos, and historical records.

Bid or Buy It Now

Make real-time bids in naira, or instantly secure a vehicle with Buy Now pricing. We handle the full auction process on your behalf.

Pay & Confirm Purchase

Make payment securely in naira.
Once confirmed, your vehicle is reserved, inspected, and prepped for shipping.

Shipping & Tracking

Your car is loaded into a container/RO-RO vessel and shipped directly from China to Lagos. Shipping updates are available in your dashboard.

Pickup in Lagos

Once your vehicle arrives, our clearing partners process it and notify you for pickup.

Verified Condition Reports
